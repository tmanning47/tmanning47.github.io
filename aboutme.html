About Me:

Travis Manning

I.T Specialist, Photographer, Musician, Share-er of stuff

Who I am & what I do:

I wear many hats! By day, you will find me providing support as an IT Support Specialist. After hours, it's time to learn & experiment. This website is my hub for sharing the things I'm passionate about. You'll find a mix of practical resources like project write-ups, configuration guides, and downloadable files to fuel your own adventures. A bit of a mix of work and play!

I'm also a music enthusiast & audiophile. Here, I'll be sharing curated playlists alongside my own original music (coming in the future!). Website will contain varying percentages of the following ingredients: Photography, art, gaming, simulators, music theory, etc.. Dive in, explore, and feel free to check out the content on any of my YouTube channels or social media!
